Payment Methods

At Opti Pharmacy, we aim to provide a secure and seamless payment experience for our customers. We accept a wide variety of payment methods to make your shopping experience easier and more convenient.

  • Debit Cards (VISA, MasterCard, etc.)
  • Credit Cards (VISA, MasterCard, American Express)
  • Google Pay (UPI)
  • PayPal
  • Stripe
  • Apple Pay
  • Bank Transfers (for larger payments)

All payment methods are secured with encryption technology to protect your personal and financial information. We ensure a smooth transaction process for our customers in both the USA and India.
